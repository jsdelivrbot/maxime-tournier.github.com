Research Interests

I'm interested in human motion in general, with an emphasis on the dynamic simulation and control of virtual human characters.

My Ph.D thesis is entitled dimension reduction for character animation. In this work, we investigate the use of non-linear, geometric statistical methods to capture a reduced description of a posture subspace from motion capture data. We present applications to real-time, physically-based character simulation and control, by working in a low-dimensional, data-driven posture subspace.
